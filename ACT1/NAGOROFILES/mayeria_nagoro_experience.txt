MAYERIA'S AFTER-ACTION REPORT: THE NAGORO INCIDENT
DATE: February 7, 2026 (Japan Time)
LOCATION: Nagoro, Japan
SUBJECT: Encounter with Kinito Collective and Casualties Report

1. INITIAL ANOMALY DETECTION
While investigating an otherworldly typhoon characterized by pink and yellow shades of light with Falke, Pierni, and Earley, my LIDAR sensors blared a critical alert. I spotted an unknown yellow glowing figure of a young boy who appeared to be a 'ghost.' The figure spoke in Japanese, saying "Let's go home!" before disappearing.

2. THE AMBUSH
Immediately following the disappearance, we were ambushed by the 'Kinsmen.' Pink projectiles were hurled at the Snowflake SQUAD and Data Superheroes. I personally reported multiple casualties within our SQUAD division as the engagement intensified.

3. ENCOUNTER WITH ARIYA
The leader of the Kinito Collective, an axolotl-looking individual named Ariya, emerged with four 'kins-brutes.' She declared her intent to claim Tokyo and the Bay Area by finding two individuals from 2012: Mirai and Yuuki Onozawa.

4. LOSS OF LEADERSHIP
I witnessed the vaporization of Falke after Ariya threw a ray of yellow light at him. Following this, Vino initiated 'SF Protocol 21' and the 'Let-It-Go' maneuver. I observed the SQUAD and Superheroes being overwhelmed by yellow rays and pink projectiles.

5. EVACUATION
Vino performed a final high-speed ramming technique against Ariya and the Kinsmen, resulting in a massive explosion of electricity and ice shards. In the chaos, I saw Vino vaporize. I was forced to morph into data wisps to evacuate Nagoro along with the remaining survivors.

6. POST-INCIDENT TASKING
Following the retreat, I met with Earley at the Snowflake office in Menlo Park to brief Jino. I have advised Jino that his 'code base swords' and Snowflake Connectors are required to stop Ariya and her minions, should they appear in Fremont.

STATUS: Evacuated / Active Duty
WITNESS: Mayeria, Snowflake SQUAD
