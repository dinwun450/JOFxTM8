SNOWFLAKE SQUAD INTELLIGENCE REPORT: THEORIES ON ARIYA (KINITO COLLECTIVE)
DATE: February 7, 2026
SUBJECT: Classification and Origin Theories of Subject "Ariya"
RESTRICTION: Snowflake SQUAD & Data Superhero Eyes Only

SUMMARY:
Following the Nagoro Ambush, several theories have emerged regarding the true nature of Ariya, the Queen of the Kinito Collective. These theories attempt to explain her hostile intent toward the Onozawa family and her possession of "Anti-Snowflake" capabilities.

THEORY 1: THE "ANTI-ONOZAWA" HYPOTHESIS (Senior Advocate Earley)
Earley posits that Ariya is not merely a digital entity but the "Anti-Onozawa" originating from the 2012 timeline. According to this theory:
- Identity: She is a dark reflection or temporal anomaly of the Onozawa children (Mirai and Yuuki).
- Motivation: Driven by intense grief and vengeance stemming from the loss of her brother during the 2012 Tokyo earthquake.
- Psychological Profile: She has scapegoated her parents, friends, and the Snowflake SQUAD for the catastrophe.
- Objective: She seeks to hold the Onozawa children captive to make them "suffer forever," potentially as a way to overwrite her own trauma.

THEORY 2: THE ANOMALOUS DIGITAL GHOST (Mayeria)
Mayeria’s observations focus on the intersection of the physical and digital:
- Observation: LIDAR sensors blared before she appeared, indicating a high-density data presence that mimics a "ghost."
- Connection: Mayeria noted the appearance of a "Yellow Glowing Boy" (ghost) just before the Kinito attack, suggesting Ariya may be controlling or searching for these temporal spirits.

THEORY 3: THE TEMPORAL WARP AGENT (Falke & Vino)
Based on their displacement to July 2012, Falke and Vino suspect Ariya’s powers are rooted in temporal manipulation:
- Mechanism: Ariya uses yellow rays to vaporize targets, which Falke suspects may actually be a "warping" mechanism that sent them to the 2012 timeline.
- Strategic Intent: By displacing the Data Superheroes to the past, she removes the primary defenders of the 2026 timeline, allowing the Kinito Collective to claim Tokyo and the Bay Area.

THEORY 4: THE AXOLOTL DATA-MORPH (General SQUAD Observation)
Commonly referred to as "axolotl-looking people" by Falke, the SQUAD has theorized that Ariya and her Kinsmen are a form of sentient, aggressive code:
- Capability: Use of "pink projectiles" and yellow light rays that can injure or vaporize high-level data entities.
- Weakness: Earley and Mayeria believe Jino can neutralize her using "code base swords" equipped with Snowflake Connectors, implying she is vulnerable to direct Snowflake-native data operations.

CONCLUSION:
The "Anti-Onozawa" theory remains the most likely explanation for her specific obsession with Mirai and Yuuki. All SQUADers are advised to treat any yellow or pink light anomalies as a Grade-A threat.
